Welcome to Save the Children South Africa

At Save the Children South Africa, we are committed to ensuring that every child in our nation has the opportunity to thrive. Our work focuses on improving the lives of the most vulnerable children, helping them to grow up healthy, educated, and safe from harm. We believe that every child deserves a future, and with your help, we can make that belief a reality.

Together, We Can Change the Future for Every Child

We work tirelessly across South Africa to protect and support children, ensuring they have access to the resources and opportunities they need to succeed. From providing quality education and healthcare to safeguarding children from abuse and exploitation, our mission is to create a lasting impact in their lives and communities.
